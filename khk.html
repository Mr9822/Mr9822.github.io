About the Super Seminar

Restigouche Dojo is proud to host a special Judo & Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu seminar in support of the Kids Helping Kids Super Seminar fundraiser. While the official Super Seminar takes place in Alberta, we are contributing to the cause by raising funds, spreading awareness, and bringing our community together for an exciting martial arts event. Join us for a day of learning, training, and making a difference.

  • Participants of all ages will receive Judo and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu training from experienced black belts.
  • Following the training, enjoy a dynamic Roll-a-thon (10 rounds of 3 minutes each) with local athletes.
  • Entry fee: $10.00.
  • To be entered into the prize draw, a minimum of $20 raised is required (includes 1 prize draw entry).
  • Additional prize draw entries for each $20 raised/donated.

Special Participation Incentives

For every $20 you raise, your name is entered into a prize draw for amazing prizes.

About Little Warriors

Little Warriors is a Canadian charity focused on the prevention, awareness, and treatment of child sexual abuse. Through programs like the Be Brave Ranch, Be Brave Bridge, and Prevent It! workshops, Little Warriors is committed to providing resources and support to affected children, teens, and families.

Additional Programs by Little Warriors

  • Be Brave Ranch: Trauma-informed treatment for children and teens impacted by abuse.
  • Be Brave Bridge: Online trauma support for affected families.
  • Prevent It! Workshop: A program that teaches adults how to prevent child sexual abuse.
