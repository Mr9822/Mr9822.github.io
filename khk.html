About the Super Seminar

Join us for the Kids Helping Kids Super Seminar 3.0, an event designed to raise funds for Little Warriors, a charity dedicated to preventing, raising awareness, and treating child sexual abuse. This year’s event promises exciting sessions and the opportunity to make a difference.

  • Participants of all ages will receive Jiu Jitsu training led by Black Belts.
  • Following the training, enjoy a 1-hour open mat rollathon with local athletes.
  • Entry fee: $20.00 (includes 1 prize draw entry).
  • Additional prize draw entries for each $20 raised.
  • Raise $200 or more to be eligible for a PS5.

About Little Warriors

Little Warriors is a Canadian charity focused on the prevention, awareness, and treatment of child sexual abuse. Through programs like the Be Brave Ranch, Be Brave Bridge, and Prevent It! workshops, Little Warriors is committed to providing resources and support to affected children, teens, and families.

Meet Hal MacDonald

Hal MacDonald is a passionate advocate for Little Warriors, inspired by his sister’s story of resilience after surviving abuse. His dedication has fueled past fundraisers, including the 60X60@60 event, where Hal raised $75,000 by sparring with 60 athletes in a single session. Last year, he helped raise $54,000 through a similar BJJ seminar.

Additional Programs by Little Warriors

  • Be Brave Ranch: Trauma-informed treatment for children and teens impacted by abuse.
  • Be Brave Bridge: Online trauma support for affected families.
  • Prevent It! Workshop: A program that teaches adults how to prevent child sexual abuse.
