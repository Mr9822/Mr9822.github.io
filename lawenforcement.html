Program Overview

Our BJJ trial class for Law Enforcement is designed to introduce officers to essential Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu principles. This class can enhance your ability to safely handle critical situations, providing techniques and strategies applicable to law enforcement scenarios. Whether you're a beginner or have martial arts experience, this class offers a positive learning experience that could benefit your career.

Meet Your Instructor: Maxime Raymond

Maxime Raymond, with over 15 years of experience on the provincial team of Judo New Brunswick and international competition experience, will lead the session. A black belt in Judo and brown belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Maxime has trained with Olympic athletes and provided training to law enforcement officers. His expertise in both disciplines ensures a class tailored to your needs as a Law Enforcement professional.

Who is This Class For?

This trial class is suitable for all individuals—whether you're a beginner or have an advanced level in martial arts. Law Enforcement officers from all backgrounds can experience the benefits of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, developing practical skills that enhance their physical and mental preparedness.

Key Components of the Trial Class

BJJ for Law Enforcement

Enhance your ability to handle critical situations safely, drawing on principles that can be translated effectively to various scenarios, including law enforcement situations. Gain skills that will give you confidence and control on the job.

Effective Use of Leverage and Technique

BJJ's focus on leverage and technique enables you to subdue and control opponents with minimal effort. This maximizes effectiveness while reducing the risk of injury. Our trial class seamlessly integrates these principles into valuable, job-related training.

Enhanced Physical and Mental Preparedness

Participate in a mentally and physically challenging training program, developing discipline, focus, and resilience. These are essential attributes for Law Enforcement professionals. Our supportive environment will help you build the mental and physical strength needed to excel in demanding situations.

Instructor's Integration Plan

Maxime Raymond has developed a specific plan to help law enforcement officers integrate seamlessly into regular BJJ classes. This plan focuses on maintaining each officer's integrity while allowing them to take control of their learning experience. Officers will be encouraged to set personal goals and work at their own pace, ensuring they feel comfortable and confident as they transition into ongoing training. This tailored approach aims to foster a positive and supportive environment where officers can grow both personally and professionally within the martial arts community.

Key Benefits of BJJ for Law Enforcement

  • Effective Subduing Techniques: BJJ provides law enforcement officers with a set of grappling techniques and defensive tactics to control and subdue suspects without causing unnecessary harm.
  • Minimal Force: BJJ emphasizes leveraging and technique over brute strength, minimizing the risk of injury to both officers and suspects.
  • Improved Officer Safety: Officers can develop skills and confidence to safely control and subdue suspects.
  • Enhanced Physical Fitness: BJJ training improves overall fitness, strength, flexibility, and endurance.
  • Stress Relief: BJJ provides relief from acute and chronic stress, beneficial for officers facing high-stress situations.
  • Community Engagement: Partnering with local BJJ academies can foster positive relationships and trust within the community.
  • Proven Effectiveness: Successfully used by law enforcement agencies, demonstrating effectiveness in real-world situations.
  • Complementary to Existing Training: BJJ can enhance existing use-of-force training programs.
