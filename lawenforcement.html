Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) Classes for Law Enforcement

Experience Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) Classes tailored for law enforcement professionals. Our specialized class focuses on practical self-defense techniques and essential skills for individuals in their careers.

Under the guidance of our highly skilled instructor, Maxime Raymond, who holds a Judo black belt and a BJJ purple belt, you'll learn fundamental BJJ techniques with practical applications relevant to law enforcement.

The class is exclusively open to law enforcement personnel, offering training suitable for participants of all fitness and experience levels.
