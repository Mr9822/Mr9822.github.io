Try Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) for Free!

Experience the fundamentals of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) in our one-time trial class, exclusively available to law enforcement professionals. This session is designed to introduce you to essential BJJ skills that can benefit you in your career.

Why Choose Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu at Restigouche Dojo?

Practical Self-Defense Techniques for Law Enforcement

Experience BJJ techniques designed to enhance your ability to handle critical situations safely, with a focus on the needs of law enforcement professionals. Our instructors are well-versed in the unique challenges faced in the field and offer effective training to address them successfully.

Effective Use of Leverage and Technique

BJJ's focus on leverage and technique enables you to subdue and control opponents with minimal effort, maximizing effectiveness while reducing the risk of injuries. Our classes for Law Enforcement professionals seamlessly integrate these principles, providing valuable training for the challenges they may encounter on duty.

Enhanced Physical and Mental Preparedness

Participate in a mentally and physically challenging training program, developing discipline, focus, and resilience, essential attributes for Law Enforcement professionals. Our classes provide a supportive environment to build the mental and physical strength needed to excel in demanding situations.
