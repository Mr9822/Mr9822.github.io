About Shawn McManus

Shawn McManus is not just a black belt – he’s a proven champion and dedicated instructor with an undeniable track record. As the 2020 Rogue Grand Prix Champion (open weight, no time limit, submission only) and the 2016 Mind Games Lightweight Champion, Shawn has established himself as a force on the mat. Known for his skill in dominating with submissions, he boasts an unbeaten record on Smoothcomp with 31 wins by submission – a feat few can claim!

Shawn's BJJ Achievements

  • Black Belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu
  • 2020 Rogue Grand Prix Champion (open weight, no time limit, submission only)
  • 2016 Mind Games Lightweight Champion
  • Certified Referee for Military Combative Grappling Championships
  • Instructor at Gagetown Submission Grappling
  • 31 Wins on Smoothcomp – All by Submission!
