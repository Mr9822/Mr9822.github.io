About the Seminar

We are thrilled to welcome Shawn McManus, known for his relentless pursuit of submissions and elite-level instruction, to Restigouche Dojo! With his unmatched expertise in both Judo and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Shawn is coming to share the techniques, insights, and strategies that have propelled him to the top. Whether you’re a beginner or an advanced practitioner, this seminar promises transformative insights that can take your BJJ journey to the next level!

BJJ
