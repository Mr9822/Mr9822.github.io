Restigouche Dojo

Mike Miller

Mike Miller is a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu blue belt with years of experience training, mentoring, and coaching young athletes. His dedication to martial arts and his ability to connect with students make him a cornerstone of Restigouche Dojo’s youth program. Mike focuses on fostering confidence, discipline, and technical proficiency, helping students grow as athletes and individuals.

Professional Expertise

With over 13 years as an instructor for the Department of Public Safety, Mike specializes in use-of-force techniques and officer safety. As a certified Police Jiu-Jitsu instructor through C4C, he integrates real-world applications into his teaching, bridging the gap between martial arts and professional safety practices.

Multi-Sport Background

Mike’s athletic journey includes competing as a professional Highland Games athlete and excelling in sports such as hockey, volleyball, and baseball. This diverse background enhances his coaching style, emphasizing versatility and adaptability.

Achievements and Certifications

  • Certified Police Jiu-Jitsu instructor through C4C
  • Over 13 years as an instructor for the Department of Public Safety
  • Restigouche Dojo Leadership Course graduate
  • Extensive experience mentoring youth athletes

Teaching Philosophy

Mike believes in creating a supportive environment where students feel empowered to challenge themselves. His teaching emphasizes technical precision, teamwork, and personal development, inspiring students to succeed both on and off the mats.

Mike Miller’s expertise and passion for teaching make him an invaluable asset to Restigouche Dojo, shaping the next generation of martial artists.
