Welcome to the Fall Prevention Workshop

Our program is designed to help senior citizens reduce their risk of falling and improve their balance and mobility, while also teaching them how to fall properly to reduce the risk of injury. Join us for a one time 75 minutes session led by an experienced instructor.

Program Benefits

  • Reduce the risk of falling
  • Improve balance and mobility
  • Learn proper falling techniques
  • Supportive community and experienced instructors
