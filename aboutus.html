About Restigouche Dojo

Our Mission

Welcome to Restigouche Dojo! Established in 2023, our mission is to share the fundamentals of martial arts with people of all skill levels. From Tiny Tots to adults, our classes in Judo, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, and self-defense build discipline, confidence, and community. Join us and experience the transformative power of martial arts — where strength, respect, and growth come together.
