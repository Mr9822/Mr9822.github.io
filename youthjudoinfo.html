Restigouche Dojo

Youth Judo Classes

Youth Judo at Restigouche Dojo is a dynamic Judo program designed for children aged 7 and above. Our program integrates elements from Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) and Wrestling to provide young learners with a comprehensive foundation in grappling arts. Our classes focus on teaching fundamental techniques and concepts of Judo while incorporating beneficial aspects from BJJ and Wrestling.

Led by experienced instructors specialized in working with youth, our Youth Judo classes offer a holistic approach to martial arts education. Children aged 7 and above will learn standing and ground techniques (ne waza and tachi waza), grappling drills (randori), breakfalls, and the core values of Judo in a safe and supportive environment.

We firmly believe that Youth Judo is a valuable tool for helping children develop physically, mentally, and socially. All of our instructors comply with Judo Canada standards and adhere to a strict code of conduct.

Enroll your child in Youth Judo at Restigouche Dojo today, and provide them with a well-rounded martial arts education that cultivates confidence, resilience, and a lifelong passion for grappling arts.
