  • What is Judo?

    "The gentle way"

    Jigoro Kano created the modern Japanese martial art of judo in 1882.

    It has developed into a global sport that is acknowledged as an Olympic sport.

  • Judo Moral Code

    1. Courage
    2. Respect
    3. Modesty
    4. Sincerity
    5. Friendship
    6. Honour
    7. Politeness
    8. Self control

  • Why Judo?

    1. For various people, judo has different meanings. It is an enjoyable sport, an art, a discipline, a social and leisure activity, a fitness regimen, a means of self-defense, and a way of life. It combines all of these things.
