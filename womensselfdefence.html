Women’s Self-Defence — Control & Connection

Enroll / Inscription

Workshop • Dates TBA

We’re excited to bring back our Women’s Self-Defence Workshop — with a new focus. In our previous session we covered prevention, creating distance, and seeking safety. This time, we dive into what happens when connection is already made. Learn the most effective Judo and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu techniques to control, immobilize, and neutralize an aggressor until help arrives. Whether you’re a beginner or experienced, you’ll build confidence and calm under pressure in a supportive, inclusive environment.

Contact Us to Enroll

What You’ll Learn

Boundary Skills

Voice, posture, frames, and simple position changes that keep you safer without escalating.

Connection Control

Clinches, off-balancing, and safe takedown options to reach a stable position.

Ground Safety

Pin escapes, frames, hip movement, and hold-downs that let you control and call for help.

Calm Under Pressure

Progressive, supportive practice that builds composure and decision-making.

Format & What to Bring

Format: short concept demos → partner drills → gentle, scenario practice with clear boundaries.

Wear: comfortable athletic clothing (no zippers). Water bottle recommended.

Who: welcoming environment; no experience required.

Disclaimer

This program does not guarantee safety. Real situations vary and require personal judgment. Our goal is to empower participants with practical skills and confidence while emphasizing de-escalation, awareness, and seeking help.
