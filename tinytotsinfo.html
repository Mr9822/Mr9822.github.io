Tiny Tots Judo Classes

Our Tiny Tots group is a "parent and me" class where one parent or caregiver will join their little one on the mats. During the 45-minute class, you can expect a warm-up, games, obstacle courses, partner activities with your child, introduction to Judo techniques, and breakfalls. This is a great opportunity for you to bond with your child while also introducing them to a healthy and active environment.

Why Choose Restigouche Dojo for Tiny Tots Judo?

Develop Coordination and Motor Skills

Tiny Tots Judo is a great way to help your child develop coordination and motor skills, which are important for their overall physical development.

Learn Important Social Skills

Our Tiny Tots Judo program emphasizes teamwork and cooperation, helping your child develop important social skills and make new friends.

Have Fun and Stay Active

Tiny Tots Judo is a fun and engaging activity that will keep your child active and energized while also providing them with important life skills.
