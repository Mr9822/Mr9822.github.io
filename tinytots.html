Tiny Tots Judo (Ages 2–4)

Register Now

Parent & Me • 35-Minute Fun Classes

Our Tiny Tots class is a welcoming “parent-and-me” experience where one caregiver joins their little one on the mats. Through play, movement, and gentle structure, we introduce the world of grappling arts — Judo, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, and Wrestling — building confidence, coordination, and connection.

What You’ll Do Together

Coordination

Fun balance drills, animal walks, and simple movement patterns that build motor skills safely.

Safety First

Foundations like gentle breakfalls to foster body awareness and confidence on the mats.

Social Skills

Short team games and partner activities that encourage taking turns, sharing, and cheering others on.

Bonding

Caregiver participation keeps kids engaged and turns learning into a shared weekly ritual.

Class Flow

Warm-up: playful movement (bear/crab walks), balance ladders, and mat awareness.

Skill of the Day: age-appropriate grappling fundamentals (e.g., safe falling, body alignment, “big steps”).

Games & Obstacle Course: tunnels, cones, and partner tasks to reinforce the day’s theme.

Cool-down & High-Fives: gratitude, stickers, and a quick recap of what we learned.
