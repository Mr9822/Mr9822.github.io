Seniors — Safe Fall Training

Enroll / Inscription

Reduce Risk. Build Confidence.

This one-time, 75-minute workshop helps older adults reduce fall risk and learn safe landing strategies. We focus on balance, mobility, and simple techniques to minimize injury — taught in a calm, supportive environment.

Reserve Your Spot

Program Benefits

Fall Risk Reduction

Simple daily habits and balance drills designed to lower the chance of falls.

Balance & Mobility

Gentle progressions to improve stability, coordination, and confidence in movement.

Safe Landing Basics

Age-appropriate, low-impact “how to fall” concepts to help reduce injury if a fall happens.

Supportive Coaching

Experienced instructors who adapt to your comfort level and individual needs.

What to Expect

Format: brief education → guided balance/mobility → safe landing concepts on soft mats.

Wear: comfortable clothing (no zippers). Indoor shoes optional.

Accessibility: all movements are scalable; we work at a careful, comfortable pace.
