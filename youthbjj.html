Discover No-Gi BJJ (Ages 7–13)

Our Youth No-Gi BJJ program builds confidence, discipline, and technical mastery in a fun, supportive environment. Classes focus on movement quality, positional awareness, and smart self-defence — no uniform required.

Program Highlights

Schedule

One-hour No-Gi classes, typically Mondays 5–6 pm. Check the schedule for current times and session dates.

Curriculum

Fundamentals from the Rodrigo Resende system, led by Coach Max (BJJ brown belt): takedowns, guard, passes, pins, escapes, and youth-appropriate submissions.

Skills

Leverage and control over strength, problem-solving under pressure, and safe situational awareness.

Community

Encouraging culture, teamwork, and leadership — learning to train hard while taking care of partners.
