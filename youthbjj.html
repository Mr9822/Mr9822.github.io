For Ages 7-13

We are thrilled to announce our Youth BJJ Program! Starting with one-hour No-Gi classes every Monday from 5-6 PM, this program introduces children aged 7-12 to the fundamentals of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in a safe, supportive, and fun environment.

Curriculum

Classes are based on the proven curriculum of Rodrigo Resende, a 5th-degree black belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, and are also under the direct instruction of Maxime Raymond, a BJJ brown belt. The curriculum focuses on foundational techniques, positional awareness, and confidence-building exercises. Students will develop skills that enhance their focus, fitness, and self-defense capabilities.
